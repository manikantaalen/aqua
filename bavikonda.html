Bavikonda trekking trail
Bavikonda trekking trail
March 16, 2018

Bavikonda trekking trail, part of the Western Ghats, is secluded, untouched and on the way are waterfalls that will rejuvenate and energize you while heading to the peak. This trek through the forest is a different experience during each season. The best thing in March is that you won’t befriend any leeches. Beginning at the foothills of the Mullodi village, it is a perfect venture for all trekkers on a weekend. First timers will not just complete it but also enjoy the trek with not much difficulty. If you want to step out before April summer sets in, this is where you can go with us.

TREK FACTS
Location : Mullodi, Kudremukh
Peak Height : 1,415m approx..
Distance : 15km both ways
Total Time : 6 – 8 hours
Level : Moderate
Trail : Greenland & slopes and waterfalls
Starting Point : Mullodi
Journey : 330 km from Bangalore

FITNESS
Even if you are not into regular fitness regime, basic exercise to keep your muscles flexible and cardio endurance is important. Climbing an inclined terrain would exert body muscles to an extent, so good stretches and exercise is advisable. If spending time outdoors is not possible, basic exercise at home for about 20 mins will suffice. On another note, regular exercise will keep your body and mind alert, in good shape and feeling good about yourself is ultimate.
